Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Introduction

Covenant Capital, operated by STH Financial LLC, respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide through our website, forms, and communication channels. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information you submit directly, including your name, phone number, email address, business name, funding needs, financial profile details, and any other information you provide through our application or partner inquiry forms. We may also collect device, browser, usage, and analytics information automatically.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to respond to inquiries, evaluate funding or partnership eligibility, connect you with products or services, deliver onboarding and support, improve our website and operations, communicate updates, and comply with legal obligations.

SMS Communications & Consent (A2P Compliance)

When you provide your phone number and opt in, you authorize Covenant Capital to send transactional, support, and marketing-related text messages related to your inquiry, funding options, partner application, or account activity. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Consent is not a condition of purchase. You may reply STOP at any time to opt out or HELP for assistance.

Mobile Information Sharing

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, except vendors or service providers that help us deliver messaging services on our behalf.

Opt-Out & Preferences

You can unsubscribe from promotional emails using the unsubscribe link provided in those emails. For SMS, reply STOP to opt out. You may also contact us directly at sthfinancialllc@gmail.com to request updates, corrections, or removal of your information where permitted by law.

Cookies & Tracking

We may use cookies, analytics tools, and similar technologies to understand traffic, improve user experience, measure campaign effectiveness, and optimize site performance. You can manage cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Data Security

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your personal information. However, no system can guarantee absolute security, and you provide information at your own risk.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools and service providers for website hosting, analytics, CRM, communication, payment processing, and fulfillment support. These providers may process information on our behalf under their own privacy and security obligations.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect legal, operational, or service changes. The revised version will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact Covenant Capital / STH Financial LLC at sthfinancialllc@gmail.com or visit https://covenantcapitalfunding.com.
